Our Story

The Journey of EcoFi

From a simple idea to a movement that's reshaping waste management across India.

The Beginning

The Idea Sparked

Walking through streets lined with overflowing bins and unsorted waste, we asked ourselves — "What if technology could solve this?" That question became EcoFi.

AI Integration

Smart Classification

We built an AI-powered waste classification system that identifies recyclable, organic, and hazardous waste with a single photo — making sorting effortless for everyone.

Community First

Building the Movement

EcoFi grew beyond tech — volunteers, donors, community drives, and awareness events created a network of eco-warriors dedicated to Swachh Bharat.

Today & Beyond

Scaling Impact

With EcoCoins, a green marketplace, event organizing, and real-time waste tracking — we're now a complete ecosystem for sustainable living.

Our Mission

To empower every individual, household, and community in India with AI-driven tools and incentives that make proper waste management easy, rewarding, and impactful.

  • Make waste sorting as simple as snapping a photo
  • Reward eco-conscious behavior with EcoCoins
  • Connect communities for collective clean-up action
  • Reduce landfill waste by 40% in target areas

Our Vision

A zero-waste India where technology bridges the gap between intention and action — where every citizen is an active participant in building a circular economy.

  • AI-optimized collection routes in every city
  • 100% recyclable product ecosystem
  • Carbon-negative operations by 2030
  • India's largest eco-conscious community
What We Do

The Four Pillars of EcoFi

01

AI Waste Classification

Upload a photo of any waste item and our trained AI model instantly classifies it — telling you exactly how to dispose of it responsibly.

02

EcoCoin Rewards

Every eco-action earns EcoCoins — from sorting waste and volunteering to attending cleanup events. Redeem them for products in our green marketplace.

03

Green Marketplace

Shop products made entirely from recycled materials. Every purchase supports waste-to-product businesses and fuels a circular economy.

04

Community & Events

Organize or join cleanup drives, awareness rallies, and workshops. Build your local eco-network and compete on the green leaderboard.
