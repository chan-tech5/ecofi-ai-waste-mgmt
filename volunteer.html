EcoFi Volunteers

EcoFi volunteers play a crucial role in transforming waste management through AI-driven solutions, community engagement, and sustainable practices. By joining our initiative, you contribute to spreading awareness, optimizing waste segregation, and promoting a circular economy. Volunteers assist in waste classification, support recycling efforts, and engage with communities to drive behavioral change. With opportunities to participate in awareness drives, tech-enabled waste tracking, and sustainable e-commerce initiatives, you help create a zero-waste future.
